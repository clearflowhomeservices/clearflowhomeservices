Why drainage & downspouts matter

Most water problems start simple: downspouts dumping too close to the home, negative grading, clogged outlets, or soil that holds water. Over time, that moisture can work its way toward the foundation, crawl space, or basement.

Common signs you should get checked

Standing water after rain • Washed-out mulch or trenches near the home • Dirt pulling away from the foundation • Musty crawl space smell • Water staining on foundation walls • Gutter overflow or downspout splash-back.

What fixes typically look like

Solutions can be as simple as extending downspouts or as involved as grading, drainage lines, catch basins, or French drains. The goal is always the same: move water away from the home and control where it exits the yard.
