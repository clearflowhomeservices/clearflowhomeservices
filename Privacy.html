Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 10, 2026

ClearFlow Home Services (“ClearFlow,” “we,” “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. By submitting information through our website or forms, you agree to this policy.

1) Information we collect

  • Contact information you provide (such as name, phone number, email address).
  • Location details (such as ZIP code, city, and relevant address area) to match you with a local contractor.
  • Project information (such as the issue you’re experiencing, photos, and notes you submit).
  • Basic website usage data (like device/browser info) that may be collected by hosting or embedded form tools.

2) How we use your information

  • To contact you and confirm details about your request.
  • To match your request with an appropriate local contractor or service provider.
  • To improve our process and provide better routing based on common issues.
  • To help prevent spam, fraud, or misuse.

3) How we share your information

The main purpose of this website is to connect homeowners with local service providers. If you submit a request, we may share your contact and project details with one or more independent contractors so they can contact you with scheduling, quotes, or next steps. These contractors are separate businesses and are responsible for their own services and communications.

4) Your choices

  • You may request that we delete your submission information by contacting us.
  • You may ask us not to share your information with additional contractors.
  • If you no longer want to be contacted, tell us and we will stop outreach where reasonably possible.

5) Data retention

We keep information only as long as needed to fulfill your request, support operations, and comply with legal obligations. We may retain limited records to prevent fraud or repeated spam submissions.

6) Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7) Third-party tools

Our website may use third-party tools (such as embedded forms) to collect submissions. Those providers may process data under their own policies.
